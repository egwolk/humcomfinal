The Process

How did RINDeX come to be?

Step • One

Brain Storming

Coming up with ideas of what kind of website I want to build for my college final project.

Step • Two

Site Mapping

Once the idea was acquired, I started out with mapping out the website(How many pages will there be, and the flow of the website).

Step • Three

Wireframing

Them proceeded to map each of the webpage’s structures and layouts. Also figured out the theme, colors, and fonts I wanted to use for the website.

Step • Four

Prototyping and Iteration

Once I got satisfied with wireframing, I then proceeded to make non-code prototypes of the website on figma. This part also includes making website assets(logos, icons, ect.).

Step • Five

Coding

The last bit is where I started to code. Started with HTML for structure, then CSS for styling, then JavaScript for functionality, and lots of running, testing, and debugging.
